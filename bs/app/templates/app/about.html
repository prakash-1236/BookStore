About Us


Our company is a public limited company with its registered and corporate office in Pune, Maharashtra, India. Established in 2024, we officially commenced operations in 2025. As one of the fastest-growing public listed companies in Pune, we are dedicated to providing a diverse collection of books across various genres, ensuring quality and accessibility for readers nationwide. Our commitment to excellence, innovation, and customer satisfaction has positioned us as a trusted name in the book industry.



Mission


We embrace modern innovations to enhance the reading experience and make books more accessible to everyone. Through our unique ‘Relationship Reading’ model, we foster a deep connection between authors, books, and readers, ensuring a more engaging literary journey. We anticipate, understand, and respond to our customers’ needs by curating high-quality books and making them available through innovative and convenient platforms.
